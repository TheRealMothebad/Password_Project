What do I check for?

Any email could be malicious! Warning signs for malicious emails include:

  • Mispelled email addresses
  • Files to download
  • Links to click
  • Requests for information

Do not click suspicious links or open suspicious files.

Make sure that any information that you send electronically would be something you would be OK posting.

What information should you share on social media?

This is inherently a personal question. From a cybersecurity standpoint, the less information the better.

Think twice before posting:

  • Your name
  • Your birthday
  • Your location (past, present, and future)

    • This information could be used to impersonate you in a phishing attack. The safest strategy is to be completely anonymous.
